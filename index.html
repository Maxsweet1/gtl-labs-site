What I Built

I run a small fruit export business called Nutridos Foods. I needed a way to pay my laborer in Colombia only after work was actually completedâ€”fruit pickup, dehydration, and shipment. So I built ProofHarvest: a blockchain-based escrow system that only releases funds when milestones are verified.

How It Works

- You agree on steps (milestones) like pickup, prep, shipment
- Worker sends photo proof for each step
- Funds are held in smart contract escrow
- You or a verifier clicks to release payment when work is done
